Software Engineering Code of Ethics

Software engineers shall commit themselves to making the analysis, specification, design, development, testing, and maintenance of software a beneficial and respected profession. In accordance with their commitment to the health, safety, and welfare of the public, software engineers shall adhere to Eight Principles. For this discussion, you will be researching one of the Eight Principles and creating an infographic to share with the class. An infographic is a collection of imagery, charts, and minimal text that gives an easy-to-understand overview of a topic.
